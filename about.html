Our History

H
From Offline Beginnings to Online Innovation: Before launching its online platform, JobsNCR was established as a traditional recruitment agency seven years ago, working primarily offline to connect employers and job seekers. Initially, it operated out of a small office in Noida, where it provided personalized recruitment services to local businesses and individuals. The team focused on understanding the specific needs of employers and offering tailored career advice to job seekers.
